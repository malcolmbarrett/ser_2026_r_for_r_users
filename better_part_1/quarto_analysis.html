Smoking and Birth Weight Analysis

Overview

This report analyzes the relationship between smoking during pregnancy and birth outcomes after restricting to mothers aged 18 years and older and excluding the records flagged during data review.

The analysis dataset includes 1960 births. There were 40 low-birth-weight births and 189 preterm births.
